Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Shipping within Pakistan takes 3-5 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, EasyPaisa, and JazzCash. For international orders, we accept credit/debit cards and bank transfers.

Can I return or exchange an artwork?

Yes, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the artwork is in original condition. Please contact us for return instructions.

Do you offer framing?

Yes, we offer professional framing services. You can choose from various frame styles during the checkout process.

How do I care for my painting?

Keep away from direct sunlight, extreme temperatures, and humidity. Clean with a soft, dry cloth only. Do not use water or chemicals.

Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Policy

All artworks are carefully packaged and shipped within 1-2 business days after order confirmation. We use professional art shipping services to ensure safe delivery.

Delivery

We deliver across Pakistan. For major cities, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. For remote areas, it may take up to 7 business days.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. The artwork must be in its original condition and packaging.

Damaged Items

If your artwork arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.
